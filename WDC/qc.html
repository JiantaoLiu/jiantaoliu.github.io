3. Zhang and Patel [9] only receives and produces 512x512 images while our comparisons are made on 640x480 images. Despite suggested by the authors, we still concern that the comparison might be unfair because their outputs are up-sampled in vertical direction. Here, we provide the comparison on both 512x512 input and output. The conclusions are the same.
